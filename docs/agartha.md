## Overview
- **Name:** Agartha — *Own Your Eco Resort & Residences*
- **By:** Modcon Builders Pvt. Ltd. ("A creation by Modcon")
- **Concept:** A 25-acre **bespoke farmhouse community** near Hyderabad — "not just a
  farmhouse project, a way of life." ~**2 acres** dedicated to a **resort + premium
  clubhouse**. Balance of luxury and nature; eco / sustainable / permaculture living.

## Why Agartha
- Peaceful living surrounded by nature; homes designed for comfort, sustainability
  and a healthy lifestyle.
- Sells **farm-land plots**; for buyers who like Modcon's construction style, Modcon
  builds them a **custom, Bali-style** farmhouse (eco-friendly, custom-designed).
- Private backyard with a **food forest**.
- Access to the resort and clubhouse.
- Community living with like-minded people.

## Landscapes
- Edible landscapes via **permaculture** principles — self-sustaining growth that
  works with natural systems to cut maintenance and resource use; a sustainable,
  productive outdoor space.

## Farmhouses
- **Edible-landscaped backyards** — your own food forest with fruits, vegetables, herbs.
- Built with **natural materials** — bamboo, mud and lime — for a sustainable,
  healthy lifestyle.

### Retreat types
- **Bamora Retreat:** bamboo structure with a mezzanine floor; natural **mud
  plaster** finish; sustainability, thermal comfort, lightweight eco-friendly build.
- **Earthlyn Retreat:** built with **CSCB bricks** (stabilised-earth bricks); natural
  materials + thoughtful design for thermally efficient, eco-friendly buildings,
  finished with traditional **lime plastering**.

## Agartha Resort & Park — amenities
