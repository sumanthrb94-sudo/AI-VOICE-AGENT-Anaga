## 1. Project overview & master plan
- **Developer / brand:** MODCON — *Building Beyond Expectations*.
- **Project:** a **4.5-acre integrated commercial + residential** project at **Thukkuguda**.
- **Zones (master plan):** entry at the **commercial zone**, **central park**, "Elur"
  (as labelled), **200-ft wide road access** with entry/exit points; clearly defined
  residential & commercial zones, wide internal roads, organised plot layouts, green
  spaces and utility areas.
- **Why it's planned this way:** smooth traffic flow, optimal space use, future-ready
  infrastructure — a structured, sustainable environment for living and business.

## 2. Residential — MODCON SYL Residences (villas)
- A biophilic residential enclave overlooking forest landscapes; serene living with
  abundant **natural light, ventilation and sunrise views** — "luxury meets nature."
- **Villas** feature **large private balconies** over lush forest views.
  _(The brochure used the word "villament"; per Modcon the product is referred to as **villas** — Anaga says villas.)_
- Highlights → advantages:
  - Low-density planning with open spaces → natural light & ventilation.
  - Biophilic design & green corridors → calm, private, community-focused living.
  - Large balconies with forest & sunrise views → thoughtfully planned layouts.

## 3. Commercial district — MODCON ONE
- **~1.5 acres** of prime commercial land; high-visibility **mixed-use** destination.
- **Offerings:** premium **retail, offices, and food & beverage** — designed for
  steady footfall and consistent demand.
- **Investment:** prime frontage / high visibility, mixed-use demand, strong rental
  & resale potential, efficient layouts, future-ready planning, long-term capital
  appreciation — a secure, scalable opportunity.

## 6. Unit specifications (construction)
- **Structure:** RCC framed structure designed to withstand **wind & seismic** loads.
- **External walls:** **200 mm** thick; sustainable **AAC / fly-ash blocks** (or
  equivalent) with chemical bonding.
- **Doors:**
  - Main door: teak / engineered-wood frame (polish or melamine spray); **veneered
    flush shutter**, **2250 × 1150 mm**, reputed-make hardware.
  - Internal doors: teak / engineered-wood frame; laminated or veneered flush shutter,
    **2250 × 950 mm**, reputed-make hardware.
- **Windows:** **UPVC** glazing system; sliding / casement shutters with mosquito-mesh
  provision; ventilators in UPVC with translucent float glass, top-hung; **lintel
  height 2250 mm**.
